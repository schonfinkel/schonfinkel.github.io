#+title: PropEr

#+begin_quote
PropEr is a tool for the automated, semi-random, [[id:3793eb94-34f8-4195-b74a-bc28c771d973][Property-Based Testing]] of
[[id:de7d0e94-618f-4982-b3e5-8806d88cad5d][Erlang]] programs. It is fully integrated with Erlang's type language, and can
also be used for the model-based random testing of stateful systems.
#+end_quote

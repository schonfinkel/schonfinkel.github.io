#+title: Isagoge

The /Isagoge/ (Greek: Εἰσαγωγή, Eisagōgḗ; /ˈaɪsəɡoʊdʒiː/) or "Introduction" to
[[id:f6906342-8457-4da5-8182-15c5978a5728][Aristotle's Categories]], written by Porphyry in Greek and translated into Latin
by Boethius, was the standard textbook on logic for at least a millennium after
his death.

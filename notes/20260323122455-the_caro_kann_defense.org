#+title: The Caro-Kann Defense
#+filetags: :chess:

#+begin_quote
The Caro-Kann can only be played against ~e4~ and it begins with ~c6~. The point of this opening from Black's perspective is to put a pawn in the center but have it protected by another
pawn - what we call a building-block opening. So, when White puts two pawns in
the center we play ~d5~.

[cite: @rozman2023win p.309 chap.11 part.2]
#+end_quote

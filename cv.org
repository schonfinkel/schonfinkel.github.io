  [[https://www.lionsos.org/][LionsOS]]. Creating a verified foundation for deploying Erlang/Gleam
  applications.

My work seeks to converge code, academic concepts and solid mathematical
foundations, leveraging the most robust aspects of Computer Science to create
resilient systems. I'm here to deliver only what is promised, with simple
abstractions and minimal overhead.
